Research

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is known for its robust research culture. Our research centers span various disciplines including engineering, medical sciences, humanities, and social sciences. We focus on solving real-world problems through collaborative research with leading organizations and global universities.

Our faculty and students are involved in cutting-edge research in areas such as artificial intelligence, sustainable energy, healthcare technologies, and robotics. The university has established numerous partnerships with industries to promote innovation and entrepreneurship.

Artificial Intelligence

Exploring advancements in AI, including machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing to solve complex problems.

Sustainable Energy

Developing innovative solutions for renewable energy and sustainable practices to combat climate change.

Healthcare Technologies

Creating advanced healthcare systems and devices for early diagnosis and effective treatment.

Robotics

Designing intelligent robotic systems for industrial, medical, and service applications.

Social Sciences

Conducting research on societal challenges and human behavior to drive meaningful change.

Quantum Computing

Exploring the potential of quantum technologies for computational breakthroughs.

Research

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is known for its robust research culture. Our research centers span various disciplines including engineering, medical sciences, humanities, and social sciences. We focus on solving real-world problems through collaborative research with leading organizations and global universities.

Our faculty and students are involved in cutting-edge research in areas such as artificial intelligence, sustainable energy, healthcare technologies, and robotics. The university has established numerous partnerships with industries to promote innovation and entrepreneurship.
